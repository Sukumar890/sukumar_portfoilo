About

Sukumar Bandreddi, born on January 11, 1971, in Razole, Andhra Pradesh, India, is a prominent Indian film director, producer, and screenwriter known for his work in Telugu cinema. He gained recognition with his directorial debut "Arya" in 2004, which was both a critical and commercial success. Over the years, Sukumar has been acclaimed for his unique storytelling and innovative direction.

Filmography

  • Arya (2004) - Director
  • Jagadam (2007) - Director
  • Arya 2 (2009) - Director
  • 100% Love (2011) - Director
  • 1: Nenokkadine (2014) - Director
  • Nannaku Prematho (2016) - Director
  • Rangasthalam (2018) - Director
  • Pushpa: The Rise (2021) - Director
  • Pushpa 2: The Rule (2024) - Director

Awards

  • Filmfare South Award for Best Director - Telugu for "Arya" (2004)
  • Nandi Award for Best Screenplay Writer for "Arya" (2004)
  • Filmfare Award for Best Director - Telugu for "Rangasthalam" (2018)
  • Sakshi Excellence Award for Best Director for "Pushpa: The Rise" (2021)
